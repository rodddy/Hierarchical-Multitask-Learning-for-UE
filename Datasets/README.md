### Dataset Availability
> The dataset supporting the findings of this study was obtained from a collaboration with a rehabilitation technology company and contains sensitive clinical information related to human subjects. The data are not publicly available at present due to confidentiality agreements and privacy restrictions.​ However, the data will be made publicly accessible after the expiration of the confidentiality period, subject to compliance with relevant ethical regulations and data sharing policies. Inquiries regarding interim access for verification purposes may be directed to the corresponding author.
